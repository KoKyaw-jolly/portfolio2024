Food Delivery App Prototype (UI/UX)

This project is a UI/UX prototype for a comprehensive food delivery app designed for end-user interaction. It includes sketches and designs that detail the core functionalities of the app, such as browsing shops and promotions, managing user accounts, viewing notifications, ordering food, and checking order history.
